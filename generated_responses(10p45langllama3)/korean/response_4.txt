**The Memory Keeper**

For years, he had been experimenting with various technologies, from advanced robotics to cutting-edge materials science. But none of his projects had ever captured his imagination quite like the one he was working on now.

Ji-Hoon's latest obsession was a device that could tap into the memories of any object it touched. He had always been fascinated by the idea of unlocking the secrets of the past, of being able to relive the experiences and emotions of those who had come before. And so, he set out to create a machine that could do just that.

Months of tireless work went into the development of the device, which Ji-Hoon had dubbed the "Memory Keeper." It was a sleek, silver box with a series of intricate circuits and sensors that hummed with energy. When activated, the device emitted a soft, pulsating glow that seemed to emanate from within.
