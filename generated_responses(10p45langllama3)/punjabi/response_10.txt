Bhool Bhulaiya ek ped tha, jiska patjhad bahut hi lamba tha aur uske phool bahut hi sundar the. Main ped ke paas gaya aur usse poocha, "Bhool Bhulaiya, aapki kahaani kya hai?"

Bhool Bhulaiya ne kaha, "Main ek aisi kahaani sunata hoon, jo aapko pasand aayegi. Main ek ladki thi, jo apne pyar ko bhool gayi thi. Main apne pyar ko kabhi nahin dekhi aur usse kabhi nahin milni thi. Lekin main apne pyar ko kabhi nahin bhool sakti thi. Main uski yaadon mein rehti thi aur uske liye har din rohti thi."

Main ne poocha, "Bhool Bhulaiya, aapne apne pyar ko kabhi nahin dekha, to aapki yaadon mein kaise rehti thi?"

Bhool Bhulaiya ne kaha, "Main apne pyar ko kabhi nahin dekhi, lekin main uski awaaz suni thi. Main uski awaaz ko sunkar apne dil mein usse milna chahti thi. Lekin main usse kabhi nahin mil sakti thi. Lekin main uski yaadon mein rehti thi aur uske liye har din rohti thi."

Main ne poocha, "Bhool Bhulaiya, aapne apne pyar ko kabhi nahin bhoolna, to aapki kahaani kya hai?"
