Ek mystical duniya hai jahan har saal ke char mahiney alag-alag tarah se manaya jaata hai. Yeh duniya "Kalpa" kehte hain, jahan har mahiney ke aane se pehle ek mahatvapoorn festival manaya jaata hai.

**Basant Mahiney ka Festival - "Udaan"**

Basant mahiney ke aane se pehle, Kalpa ke log "Udaan" festival manate hain. Yeh festival udne ki shuruaat ke liye hai, jab prakriti naya jeevan lekar aati hai. Log apne gharon ko saaf karte hain, aur phoolon aur patton ko khilaya jaata hai. Festival ke dauraan, log apne gharon ke aas-paas ek bada park banaate hain, jahan unke ghar ke log ek dusre ke saath milte hain aur khelte hain.

**Garma ke Festival - "Agni"**

Garma ke mahiney ke aane se pehle, Kalpa ke log "Agni" festival manate hain. Yeh festival aag ke liye hai, jab prakriti apne garmi se logon ko bachaane ke liye aag ko prashikshit karti hai. Log apne gharon ko aag se bachaane ke liye tayar karte hain, aur festival ke dauraan, log aag ke khel khelte hain, jaise ki aag ke khel, aag ke gane, aur aag ke natakon.

**Sarad ke Festival - "Varsha"**

Sarad ke mahiney ke aane se pehle, Kalpa ke log "Varsha" festival manate hain. Yeh festival barish ke liye hai, jab prakriti apne logon ko paani se bharna ke liye barish karti hai. Log apne gharon ko saaf karte hain, aur festival ke dauraan, log barish ke khel khelte hain, jaise ki barish ke gane, barish ke natakon, aur barish ke khel.
