Ek purani library thi, jahan har kitab ek alag-alag duniya mein le jaati thi. Main ek shaukeen padhak tha, aur maine ek kitab dekhi jiska naam tha "Aag ka Raja" (The King of Fire).

Maine kitab ko uthaya aur uski pustak par chhupaaya, aur tabhi maine ek aisa anubhav kiya jo main kabhi nahin kiya tha. Main ek aag ke raja ke darbar mein pahunch gaya, jahan aag ke raja ke paas ek bahut hi shaktishali aag thi.

Aag ka raja mujhe dekha aur mere saath baat ki. Vah mere se puchha, "Tum kya chahte ho?" Maine kaha, "Main aapki duniya mein khelna chahta hoon." Aag ka raja ne kaha, "Toh tumko ek chunauti deta hoon. Tumko meri aag se ladna hoga."

Maine kaha, "Main ladunga." Aag ka raja ne kaha, "Toh tumko meri aag ke saath ladna hoga. Agar tum jeet jate ho, toh tumko meri duniya ka ek aur raja banaya jayega."

Maine aag ke raja ki aag ke saath ladna shuru kiya. Main uski aag se lad raha tha, lekin main uski aag se nahin harta tha. Main uski aag ko samajhne ki koshish kar raha tha, aur tabhi maine dekha ki aag ke raja ki aag uski duniya ko jeevan dene ke liye thi.

Maine aag ke raja ki aag ke saath ladne ke baad, maine jeeta. Aag ka raja ne mujhe meri duniya ka ek aur raja banaya. Maine apne desh vapsi kiya aur apne logon ko aag ke raja ki aag ke baare mein bataya.

Maine kitab ko wapas library mein rakha aur socha ki main kabhi nahin bhoolunga is anubhav ko.
